Venkat Chem Record.exe

Ever needed to write your chemistry record (inorganic salt analysis) but felt frustrated? This application is for you, who wants to write your chemistry record without the frustation. I believe that writing chemistry record is tiring due to decision fatigue, and my application removes that decision making fatigue by showing you only what you need to write and no clutter...!

Salient Features include:

  • Light and Dark mode
  • any Font Face and any Font SIZE
  • Progress bar to motivate you
